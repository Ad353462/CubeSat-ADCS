ADCS_SunVecEst.m contains all code needed to estimate the reference sun vector from EARTH to SUN in the ECI coordinate system. The values needed for validation are included in the script and are specific to the time and date provided. All textbook chapters referenced in the document are included in this zip file. Full textbooks for Curtis and Markley are available on the SharePoint.

Expect magnitude errors on the order of 10^3 kilometres in magntiude and 0.X degrees in angle.

If you want to try an assortment of times and dates once the validation case is working, send them to me as a list and I will email you the data you need.
